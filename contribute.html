There are a few methods to share your presentation. You may:
  1. email a copy of your presentation to me, or
  2. if the file is too large, export your presentation to HTML and send that over.
How do I export it to HTML?
  • In keynote, go to File > Export to > HTML. Click Next. Save it somewhere easy to find (it will save as a folder containing 2 files). Email them to me.
  • In powerpoint, it's probably very similar.
